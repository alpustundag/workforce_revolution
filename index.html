ğŸ¯ 8. Strategic Recommendations

A. Short-Term Priorities (2025-2028)

Urgent Actions

  1. Skill Transformation Programs: Launch national programs for reskilling and upskilling on a sectoral basis (2-3% of GDP)
  2. AI Governance Framework:Urgently establish regulatory infrastructure (algorithmic accountability, data protection, ethical standards)
  3. Human-in-the-Loop Design: Mandatory human oversight mechanisms in critical sectors (health, finance, defense)
  4. Bias Detection and Correction: Systemic bias scanning and correction protocols in all AI systems
  5. Cybersecurity Investment: Defense systems and capacity building for AI-based threats

B. Medium-Term Strategies (2028-2033)

Structural Transformations

  1. Education Reform: Curriculum focused on AI literacy, critical thinking, and creativity in primary and secondary education
  2. Co-Responsibility Models: Sectoral standardization of human-AI joint decision-making mechanisms
  3. Social Safety Net: Strengthened social protection systems for groups experiencing job loss
  4. International Cooperation: Global standards and agreements for AI governance
  5. Sustainability Integration: AI carbon footprint reduction targets and green AI standards

C. Long-Term Vision (2033-2040)

Paradigm Shift

  1. Renewal of the Social Contract: Redefinition of the concepts of work, income, and meaning
  2. Universal Basic Income: Alternative income models to compensate for automation-induced job losses
  3. Cultural Impact Assessment: Systematic analysis of AI's impact on societal values and culture
  4. Global Governance: International institutions that oversee the common good of AI for humanity
  5. Human-Centric Technology: Guaranteeing that AI serves human well-being, freedom, and dignity
